Firewall Friday — Cyber Games

🛡️ Secure or Suspect?

  • Secure ✅ – VPN encrypts your connection, making public Wi-Fi safer for work.
  • Suspect 🚫 – Using the same password increases the damage if one account is hacked.
  • Suspect 🚫 – Unexpected order emails with links are a classic phishing trick.
  • Suspect 🚫 – No screen lock means anyone can access your data if your phone is lost or stolen.
  • Suspect 🚫 – QR codes can hide malicious links; only scan from trusted sources.
  • Secure ✅ – Official app stores reduce (but don’t completely remove) the risk of malware.
  • Suspect 🚫 – Attachments can hide malware; always scan before opening.

🔤 Cipher Scramble Answers

  • YPRWESA → SPYWARE — Software that secretly collects your data without consent.
  • TBOETN → BOTNET — A network of infected devices controlled by an attacker.
  • KTOORIT → ROOTKIT — Stealthy toolkit that hides an attacker’s presence and provides hidden admin access.
  • GLEYKROEG → KEYLOGGER — Records every keystroke you type, often used to steal credentials.
  • FOSNOPIG → SPOOFING — Forging a site, email, or caller ID to look legitimate and trick victims.
  • MRHAPGIN → PHARMING — Redirecting you to a fake website even if you typed the correct address.
  • BOSXNAD → SANDBOX — Isolated environment used to safely test suspicious files or programs.
  • GPAHTCIN → PATCHING — Applying updates that fix software security weaknesses.
  • PUKABC → BACKUP — Safe copy of your important data stored in a separate location.
  • FOMTALCRUIT → MULTIFACTOR — Extra verification beyond a password, such as a code or biometric scan.

🕵️ Cyber Detective!

🕵️‍♂️ Case Closed: A hacker tricked Aarav into revealing his login credentials through social engineering and phishing. Because Aarav reused passwords and didn’t enable 2FA, the hacker got full access.

🔐 Lesson of the Week:
• Never click suspicious links, even from friends.
• Avoid using public Wi-Fi without a VPN.
• Use different passwords for every account.
• Turn on 2FA for important accounts like email and banking.
