  • 4. Rajat: 🔴 Suspect – Random links can lead to malware — always verify before clicking.

🕵️ Cyber Detective!

🕵️‍♂️ Case Closed: A hacker tricked Aarav into revealing his login credentials through social engineering and phishing. Because Aarav reused passwords and didn’t enable 2FA, the hacker got full access.

🔐 Lesson of the Week:
- Never click suspicious links, even from friends.
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi without a VPN.
- Use different passwords for every account.
- Turn on 2FA for important accounts like email and banking.
