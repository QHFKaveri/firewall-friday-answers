Firewall Friday — Answer Key 📝

🔐 Decode the Emoji – Answers

  • 1️⃣ 🧑‍💻🎭💻 → Man-in-the-Middle Attack
    👉 Hacker secretly intercepts communication between two parties.
  • 2️⃣ 🐟📧 → Phishing
    👉 Fake emails/messages to trick users into revealing sensitive info.
  • 3️⃣ 🔑🔒🔑 → Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
    👉 Adds an extra layer of protection beyond just a password.
  • 4️⃣ 🐎💾 → Trojan
    👉 Malware disguised as legitimate software to gain access.
  • 5️⃣ 🌐🔐🚪 → VPN (Virtual Private Network)
    👉 Creates a secure, encrypted tunnel for safe online browsing.
  • 6️⃣ 📂🗑️🧹 → Data Wiping
    👉 Proper erasure of files so they can’t be recovered later.
  • 7️⃣ 📡🛑📱 → Firewall
    👉 A barrier that blocks malicious or unauthorized network traffic.

🎣✅ Phish or Legit? – Answers

  • 1️⃣ “Your Netflix account has been suspended. Click here to restore access immediately.”
    👉 Phish 🎣 – Scammers use urgency + fake links to steal login/payment info.
  • 2️⃣ “Your bank never asks for your OTP on phone calls. If someone asks, it’s a scam.”
    👉 Legit ✅ – Banks educate customers about fraud; OTPs are never requested on calls.
  • 3️⃣ “Hey, here are the meeting notes from today. [Company internal link]”
    👉 Legit ✅ (if the source is trusted) – Safe if shared internally, but always verify sender.
  • 4️⃣ “You’ve won a free iPhone! Claim it now by paying ₹99 shipping.”
    👉 Phish 🎣 – Classic prize scam, designed to steal money or card info.
  • 5️⃣ “Your antivirus subscription will expire tomorrow. Renew via the official app.”
    👉 Phish 🎣 (in most cases) – Renewal alerts should only come from official apps/sites.
